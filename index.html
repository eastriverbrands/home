About East River Brands

East River Brands, LLC is a New York City based company headquartered in the wonderful, vibrant neighborhood of Astoria, Queens. Founded in the middle of 2014, East River Brands is focused on creating incredible, specialty-targetted brands primarily in the eCommerce space. Our aim is to create brands that stand apart from the rest with strong emphasis on exquisite product selection and exceptional customer service.
